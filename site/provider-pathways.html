Practitioner road

Purpose: provide integrative therapy and supervise facilitators / sessions.

1Entry credential

Current, valid New Mexico license from a recognized board that allows counseling, mental-health therapy, or behavioral-health therapy within scope.

6/12 recommendation, p. 1
2Practitioner curriculum

New Mexico module, BLS / CPR / first aid, medical monitoring, psychedelic emergencies, simulated patient module, test.

3Practicum: 120 hours

Draft says 80 administration / same-day therapy hours, 20 preparation / integration hours, plus 20 hours supervising facilitators. The practicum requirement was carved out.

420 supervisory hours challenged

6NMDOH permit

Apply, receive, and maintain a Practitioner permit.

7Continuing education

Reciprocity lane

For Practitioner or Facilitator applicants trained in another government-approved psilocybin program.

  • Provide documentation of the outside program.
  • Complete the New Mexico module.
  • Show required practicum or verified equivalent practice.
  • Open: Dec. 31, 2026 reduced-practicum date and Dec. 31, 2027 waiver authority date.

Supervisor / instructor area

This is not a defined permit in the provided draft text. It is an unsettled discussion area around who can supervise practicum or teach.

  • Proposed: practicum instructor experience with about 40 unique patients.
  • Open verification: educational-program faculty 40 individual patients.

Support roles

Healing Centers and Educational Programs support the road rather than create a separate provider permit path in this map.

  • Practicum hours take place at an approved Healing Center.
  • Educational Programs deliver curriculum, records, mentoring, and test-out options.
