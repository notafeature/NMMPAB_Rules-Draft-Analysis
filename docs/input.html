Why now. On July 17 the board voted 7-0 to send the practicum and didactic hours to the Training and Education Committee, which meets this afternoon to develop updated standards. Larry Leeman has suggested the committee bring in outside experts for that discussion, as the end-of-life committee did, ideally people with real experience and no commercial conflict of interest, and named the California Institute of Integral Studies as one example. If you know someone who could present, or have a resource or idea that helps, add it here.
How this is used. Submissions are collected and forwarded to the department together, in context, rather than one at a time. They may also be shared with a board member who asks to receive them. A comment may also be published on this site after review; by default anything published is de-identified, and it is attributed to your name only if you check the box above. Nothing is published automatically, and sending something is not an endorsement of it. A name or email is optional; your email is never published and is used only to follow up.
