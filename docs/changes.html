New Mexico Medical Psilocybin Program · Advisory Board

The rules draft, section by section

Updated July 17, 2026. This tracks the July 9 draft, section by section. The July 17 board changes (the certifying-clinician exam language and the hours deferral) are on History and the CS-number page, and are not yet in a published draft document.

This page tracks the current rules draft (July 9, 7.35.3), the text the Advisory Board went through section by section at its July 9 meeting. Each provision below shows the current text, where it came from, and whether it is settled or still open. The path that produced this draft, the June 12 committee recommendation and the June 26 board vote, is recorded further down the page. The three tabled items are tracked separately.

The current draft · 104 provisions

Section by section, in the draft's own order

Every provision below is grouped under the July 9 draft's own section headings. Each one shows the current text side by side with where it came from, so you can see what is new, what changed, what carried over untouched, and what the June 12 recommendation had that did not make it into this draft. Page numbers link into the source PDFs.

New means it first appears in the July 9 draft. Changed means the language moved from the recommendation. Carried over means it is unchanged from the recommendation. Not carried over means the recommendation had it and the July 9 draft does not. The count on each section header is a per-section breakdown of these four.
