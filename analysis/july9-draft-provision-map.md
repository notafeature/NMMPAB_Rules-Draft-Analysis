# Section-by-section changelog: June 12, 2026 committee recommendation to July 9, 2026 department rules draft (7.35.3)

Sources:
- June 12 committee recommendation: rec-0612.txt (9 pages), cited below as "rec"
- July 9 department rules draft 7.35.3: draft-0709.txt (17 pages), cited below as "draft"

Page numbers refer to the [PAGE N] markers in each extraction. Entries are grouped under the July 9 draft's own section headings, in the draft's order. A final group lists recommendation content with no counterpart anywhere in the draft.

## Preliminary sections (7.35.3.1 through 7.35.3.6)

### Rules-format preliminary sections (issuing agency, scope, statutory authority, duration, effective date, objective)
- status: new in draft
- recommendation: no counterpart
- draft: "This rule applies to all patients, practitioners, clinicians, facilitators, healing centers and other approved locations, and educational programs who participate and seek to participate in the New Mexico medical psilocybin program." (p. 1); "promulgated pursuant to the following statutory authorities: the New Mexico Department of Health Act, Subsection E of Section 9 -7-6 NMSA 1978; and the Medical Psilocybin Act, Section 26-2D-7, NMSA 1978." (p. 1)
- note: Several later sections carry the citation line "[7.35.2.x NMAC - N, x/xx/2026]" although the part is numbered 7.35.3 (pp. 5, 6, 7).

### Rename of "diagnostic clinician" to "certifying clinician"
- status: changed
- recommendation: "Diagnostic Clinician: Qualifications required to certify patients for the medical psilocybin program" (p. 1)
- draft: "the certification of certifying clinicians, practitioners, facilitators, and approved locations" (p. 1)
- note: The draft's patient application section uses the phrase "certified Diagnosing Clinician" once (p. 10).

### Definitions of "practitioner" and "facilitator"
- status: not carried over
- recommendation: "Practitioner: means an individual who is a licensed healthcare professional who is certified by the department to provide medical psilocybin integrative therapy, supervise facilitators, supervise administrative sessions, supervise same-day therapy sessions, and who has completed department required trainings." (p. 1); "Facilitator: an individual who has completed training and education approved by the department to be able to assist practitioners during the administration sessions and who has been registered with the department." (p. 1)
- draft: no counterpart (the July 9 draft contains no definitions section)

- status: new in draft

- draft: "If approved, the approval shall take effect on the date of the notice and shall remain valid for two years. ... - status: new in draft

## Practitioner and facilitator practicum requirements (7.35.3.X)

### Total practicum hours (100 facilitator, 120 practitioner)
- status: changed

- draft: "The practicum shall consist of a minimum of 100 hours of supervised practice training for facilitators and 120 hours for practitioners and must be completed prior to applying for certification." (p. 5)
- note: Hour totals are identical; the draft adds the clause requiring completion before applying for certification.

### 80 hours of administration and same-day therapy sessions (14 patients, 8 sessions)
- status: carried over unchanged
- recommendation: "a minimum of eighty (80) hours of administration and same-day therapy sessions, where students are provided the opportunity to experience, observe, or conduct supervised facilitation of administration and therapy sessions in-person with a minimum of fourteen (14) different patients over a minimum of 8 different administration and same-day therapy sessions" (p. 7)

- status: carried over unchanged
